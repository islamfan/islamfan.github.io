When asked about healthy eating in a recent interview, famous Martial Arts professional and action movies star Jean-Claude Van Damme said follow the example of Prophet Muhammad.

“The Prophet Muhammad was very smart, he knew what’s good for the future, about the body. So, look at that, don’t ask me and believe me, you have everything that you need.”
