The BBC has reported on Pakistani Muslim farmers using their time and money to build a church for the Christians in their village.

In the video a Muslim farmer finishes his morning prayers at the Mosque then he goes to the Church site. There he finds many other Muslims helping with the construction of the Church.

Ijaz Farooq a member of the local Muslim community said "Building this church, we want to show that we are untied as a community"

Faryal Masih, a member of the local Christian community and a childhood friend of Farooq said "We live with love, attend each other’s weddings and festivals, We are together in times of blessings and grief."
